Let Me Hear Your Voices

Date Wed 03 November 2010 Tags english

[caption id=”attachment_1235” align=”alignleft” width=”225” caption=”Speakup”][/caption]

This weekend I’ll be attending my first Boston Summit, courtesy of theGNOME Travel Committee! It will also be my very first time meeting my fellow members of the GNOME Foundation Board of Directors, which makes me very excited!

I wanted to ask you, readers of my blog, what kind of questions would you like me to ask on your behalf? Do you have any constructive comments or feedback for us? What are the areas you feel that we should invest more of our resources on and which ones should we spend less?

I’m also very interested to hear from other projects out there (Hi KDE, XFCE, LXDE, Openbox et all!) how can we work better together and improve our relationship?

Mind you, these are my questions alone and represent some of my personal goals if I were ever elected to the Board. My ultimate goal is to hear everyone’s opinion and start discussions with the Board this weekend.

I look forward to hearing back from you!
